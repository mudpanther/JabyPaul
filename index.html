Meet Dr. Jaby K. Paul

Born and raised in New York, Dr. Paul brings a unique blend of comprehensive expertise and cutting-edge innovation to modern dentistry.

After completing a seven-year accelerated program at the University of Medicine and Dentistry of New Jersey (now Rutgers), he refined his skills at Brooklyn Hospital Center, where he developed his passion for comprehensive care.

"I believe in finding the root cause of dental issues through great communication and shared decision-making, not just providing temporary solutions."
‚Äî Dr. Jaby K. Paul

Dr. Paul's philosophy is simple: technology-enhanced, patient-centered care that creates lifelong relationships. His expertise spans cosmetics, endodontics, oral surgery, and prosthodontics.
